// FAQ

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know.

What makes a lead "quote-ready"? +
Every lead goes through a custom qualification funnel specific to their project type. They answer questions about budget, timeline, location, and project details before we verify their contact info. By the time you receive the lead, they're expecting your call and ready for a quote.
How does the verification work? +
After completing the qualification funnel, we send a PIN code to both their email and mobile number in real-time. They must enter the correct PIN to submit their details. This ensures every contact is valid, reachable, and belongs to a real person — no bots, no fake numbers, no time wasted.
Are leads really exclusive? +
Yes. 100%. Every lead is sold to one contractor only — you. We never share, resell, or recycle leads. When you buy a lead, it's yours exclusively. The homeowner will only receive your quote, giving you the best chance to win the job.
What if I can't contact a lead? +
We guarantee 100% contact rates. Pre-qualified, in-area leads. Order 10 leads, get 10 contactable leads. Every number and email is verified by our system before delivery.
What if leads aren't delivered? +
If we don't deliver your leads within the month, you get a full refund, no questions asked. This is our commitment to you: we either deliver what we promise, or we refund you completely. You take zero risk.
Can I change my lead type or pause? +
Absolutely. You can change your lead type, adjust your volume, or pause your subscription at any time. Just contact us and we'll make the change immediately. There are no lock-in contracts or cancellation fees.
